General Sources

I checked out a few books from the CSUSB library. Particularly to help out with the earlier Soviet missions, as it's difficult to find a reliable Russian space history resouce on the internet (aside from RussianSpaceWeb, which I'm not too confident in).

Books

Beyond Earth: A Chronicle of Deep Space Exploration, 1958-2016 by Asif A. Siddiqi

Handbook of Soviet Lunar & Planetary Exploration by Nicholas L. Johnson (1979).

Robotic Exploration of the Solar System: Part 1: The Golden Age 1957-1982 by Paolo Ulivi with David M. Harland (2007).

Space Mission Art by Luke Wesley Price (2019).
