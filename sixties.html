MARINER

Mariner was a program focused on exploring our planetary neighbors Venus and Mars. Mariners 1, 2, and 5 were set for Venus while Mariners 3, 4, 6, and 7 were Mars-bound.

Most notably:

Mariner 4 sent back the first close-up views of Mars. It's also the mission where JPL scientists drew the image of the surface themselves using the data from the spacecraft, since the image processing of the time took a while.

Mariners 6 & 7 were twin spacecraft, and were able to take more pictures of the surface of Mars. Mariner 7 in particular was hit with something, suspected to be a micrometeroid, but was still able to complete its mission.
