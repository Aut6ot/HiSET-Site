Summary

In the 70s NASA looked out to the outer planets, with Pioneers 10, 11, and Voyager 1 & 2. Inward, there was progress with Venus & Mars exploration-- but more so, the Soviets making progress with Venus, and the Americans making progress with Mars. With Explorer 49, NASA moved away from lunar exploration, which would last for 21 years.

And a new thing we will see in the 80s- the Space Shuttle.
